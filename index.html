Admin Portal

An admin portal in an online voting system is a secure web-based interface that allows authorized personnel to manage and oversee the election process. The portal is usually accessible only to designated administrators, who can perform various tasks such as start or stop vote, edit candidate and voter list, complete the registration process, display candidate and voter list along with their hashcode and a lot more.

Admin Portal

Candidate Portal

A candidate portal in an online voting system is a web-based interface that allows candidates to manage their campaign, view election information, and interact with voters. The portal is only for authorized candidates, who can perform various tasks such as register themselves as candidates, view the candidate list, view result, and voting status, i.e., whether its ongoing or over or has not started.

Candidate Portal

Voter Portal

The voter portal is a critical component of an online voting application, as it provides a secure, accessible, and convenient platform for voters to participate in the election process. It enables voters to access information, resources, and support services to help them navigate the voting process, and to ensure that their vote is counted and recorded correctly.
